Tribute to Albert K Okemwa

About Albert K Okemwa

Albert K Okemwa is a renowned figure known for his significant contributions in his field. Born and raised in Kenya, he has dedicated his life to advancing knowledge and fostering innovation. His work has had a profound impact on both local and international communities.

Throughout his career, Albert has been recognized for his dedication, expertise, and hardwork.

Achievements

  • Developed Kenya’s first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led Kenya's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "Kenya 2020"
  • Received the Order of the Golden Heart, Kenya's highest civilian award
